Competition Rules

  • Each person picks 3 golfers.
  • You must have the tournament winner in your picks to win the pool.
  • If multiple players have the winner, the next best golfer breaks the tie.
  • If still tied, the 3rd golfer decides.
  • If nobody picks the winner, the prize jackpots to the next major.
